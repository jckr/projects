Interactive models > Colonel Blotto's model

Colonel Blotto's model pits two forces in a battlefield. The field is divided in a number of fronts. To ensure victory, one side must overcome the other on a majority of fronts.
So, a weaker force may triumph over a stronger one by allocating its ressources strategically, and by creating new fronts.
Here blue is stronger, but spreads its forces evenly across fronts. Can red triumph, or at least win a number of fronts?
